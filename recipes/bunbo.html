Mon Bun bo Hue

Bun bo Hue is a famous traditional dish origined from Hue City in the central of Vietnam. The dish is considered more colorful and flavorful in compare to Pho

Ingredient:

  • 2kg xuong ong heo
  • 700g bap gio heo
  • Ot xanh
  • Gung
  • Cha la Hue
  • Bap bo
  • Sa
  • Hanh tay
  • Bun bo tuoi

Steps:

  1. So che nguyen lieu, rua xuong ong heo,...
  2. Nau nuoc dung
  3. Cho hanh, rau cu vao
  4. Nuoc soi thi cho bap bo, bap gio heo, xuong vao
  5. Vot hanh tay, rau cu ra
  6. Nem nem gia vi
  7. Trinh bay va thuong thuc
